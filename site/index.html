This calculates. It doesn't divine.

Kairos is electional astrology: timing for things you plan to do, scored against your own birth chart.

What Kairos is

  • +

    A timing engine for planned actions, from signing a contract to scheduling surgery

  • +

    Scored against your own birth chart, not a generic forecast

  • +

    Rule-based and specific: every score comes from named astrological rules

  • +

    Ranked results you can act on, with a plain-language reason for each

What it isn't

  • × A daily horoscope
  • × Personality readings
  • × Vague vibes
  • × Notifications about who you are

The smart layer

Describe it in your words.

Kairos reads a plain-language request and finds the rule set that actually fits, even when it isn't the obvious one. When nothing preset matches, it writes a new rule set for your case.
