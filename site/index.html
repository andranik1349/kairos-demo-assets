Before you download.

Is this a horoscope app? +

No. Kairos doesn't describe your personality or your day. It scores the timing of things you're planning to do, using electional astrology: rules about when to begin something.

Do I need to know astrology? +

No. You describe the activity in plain words. Kairos returns scored time windows and a plain-language reason for each. The rules stay under the hood.

What do the scores mean? +

When Kairos searches for times, higher is better: a +17 window beats a +10. When it evaluates dates you already have, each gets a score from -20 to +20, so you can see at a glance which of your options is workable and which to avoid.
